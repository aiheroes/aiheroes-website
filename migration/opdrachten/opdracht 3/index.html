🌍 The Original Assignment: Cross Cultural Communication

📝 Assignment Description

Describe the country you were tasked to investigate using the three theories we discussed during the workshops (Hofstede, Trompenaars and Hall).

📊 Requirements

Touch upon the following:

  • Cultural Dimensions: Identify and highlight at least 4 dimensions from Hofstede or Trompenaars (or a mix of the two) which play a significant role in the culture of your target country.
  • Real-Life Examples: Provide examples for all 4 dimensions of real-life behaviours or practices in a professional setting that reflect these dimensions.
  • Context Classification: Classify the target country as high-context or low-context and justify your classification.
  • Comparative Analysis: Compare these traits to your country of origin and highlight potential conflicts or misunderstandings that could arise.
  • Advisory Plan: Come up with a detailed advisory plan to prevent these conflicts or misunderstandings from happening (as much as possible).

🎓 Theories to Apply

  • Hofstede's Cultural Dimensions
  • Trompenaars' Model of National Culture Differences
  • Hall's High-Context and Low-Context Cultures
