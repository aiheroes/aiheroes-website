<p>Claude 3 Sonnet: Balancing Performance and Efficiency</p>
<p>The Goldilocks model – not too big, not too small, but just right. It delivers solid performance at a reasonable cost, outshining GPT-3.5 while undercutting GPT-4's hefty price tag. For businesses seeking an AI sweet spot, Sonnet hits the mark.</p>
<p>Claude 3 Haiku: Prioritising Speed and Responsiveness</p>
<p>Your ultra-fast choice – near-instant responses and dirt-cheap costs. The standout features are its ability to excel in niche and complex domains, such as code evaluation, multilingual mathematics, and graduate-level reasoning. Anthropic has also introduced context length upgrades for all models, allowing them to handle up to 200,000 tokens. <p>One area where Claude 3 truly shines is the quality of its context recall. In a "Needle in a Haystack" test to evaluate this aspect. In this test, the model is bombarded with numerous pages of documents, and a single unrelated statement is cleverly hidden within the text. To answer accurately, Claude 3 must comprehend the entire context, including the sneaky hidden statement. And you know what? Claude 3 aces this test with flying colours, showcasing its ability to process and retain vast amounts of information</p>
<p>While Claude 3 has the best context recovery and handling capabilities we’ve seen, it has challenges.
