Search for "AI consultancy Netherlands" and you find lists full of foreign firms or thinly veiled ads. This is our version: the Dutch agencies we actually encounter in practice, with an honest characterisation. In the interest of transparency: we put ourselves at number 1, it is our list after all, but the descriptions of the others are sincere and so is the decision guide at the bottom.

## The list

### 1. AI Heroes (Groningen)

Full-service AI agency with three pillars under one roof: training, consulting and software. Strong across the whole path from first workshop to working custom solution, with published prices and proven results (70% cost reduction at <a href="/en/cases/medux">Medux</a>, prototype in 6 days at <a href="/en/cases/trabu">Trabu</a>). European focus: data sovereignty and AI Act compliance are built into the approach. Less obvious for: enterprise programmes needing dozens of consultants at once.

### 2. DataNorth AI (Groningen)

Northern AI consultancy with a strong content engine and broad services, from advice to implementation. Publishes a lot and is visible in the region.

### 3. Xomnia (Amsterdam)

One of the oldest data and AI firms in the Netherlands. Strong in data engineering and machine learning for larger organisations, including secondment of data professionals.

### 4. BigData Republic (Utrecht)

Technical heavyweight for data-intensive organisations. Strong in MLOps and production-grade machine learning for enterprise clients.

### 5. ai.nl (Utrecht)

Primarily strong in AI training with a broad open programme and many tool-specific courses. A logical starting point if you only need training without an advisory or build track.

### 6. DEUS (Amsterdam)

AI consultancy at the intersection of design and technology, with international clients. Strong where user experience weighs as heavily as the model behind it.

### 7. Enjins (Amsterdam)

Machine learning for scale-ups and mid-sized companies, focused on production-grade ML products.

### 8. Eraneos (Amsterdam)

International management and technology consultancy with a serious AI practice. Fits large organisations embedding AI in a broader transformation programme.

### 9. AI Consultancy Group (Groningen)

Small agency focused on practical AI adoption for SMEs and educational institutions in the Northern Netherlands.

### 10. Het AI Bedrijf (nationwide)

SME-focused agency for accessible AI implementations and automations.

## AI consultancy in the Northern Netherlands

Notable about this list: three of the ten firms are based in Groningen. That is no coincidence. With the AI Factory (EUR 200 million of AI infrastructure, operational from 2026-2027), the universities and a growing AI ecosystem, <a href="/en/services/ai-agency-groningen">Groningen has become the AI Capital of Europe</a>. For international firms scouting the Dutch AI scene, it is the region to watch.

## Decision guide

- **You do not know where to start:** pick a firm with an affordable entry point, like a <a href="/en/services/consulting#ai-readiness-scan">readiness assessment</a>, rather than a big programme straight away.
- **You only need training:** ai.nl for open enrolment; <a href="/en/services/incompany-ai-training">AI Heroes</a> for tailored in-company sessions.
- **You run an enterprise data landscape:** Xomnia, BigData Republic or Eraneos.
- **You want advice through working software from one partner:** that is exactly what full-service agencies like AI Heroes exist for.

Questions about what fits your situation? <a href="/en/services/ai-agency-netherlands">Plan a no-strings introduction</a>; honest advice, even when that advice is that another firm fits you better.
