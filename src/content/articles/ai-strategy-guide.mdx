## Your AI journey starts here

Many organizations know AI offers opportunities but don't know where to begin. This guide gives you a clear step-by-step plan. It's the same approach we use with our clients.

<div class="mb-12 not-prose">
  <div class="flex gap-4 mb-4 items-center">
    <div class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-brand-red text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-lg">1</div>
    <h3 class="text-xl font-serif text-brand-dark">Education</h3>
  </div>
  <p class="text-stone-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">The first step is understanding what AI can do. Not by reading for hours, but by experiencing it. Follow news about what other companies are doing, but more importantly: experiment yourself.</p>
  <div class="bg-stone-50 p-6 border-l-4 border-brand-red">
    <ul class="space-y-2 text-stone-600 list-disc ml-5">
      <li><strong>Follow AI articles:</strong> LinkedIn, tech news sites, our newsletter</li>
      <li><strong>Experiment:</strong> Use ChatGPT for your daily tasks</li>
      <li><strong>Train your team:</strong> A workshop provides hands-on experience that reading can't</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mb-12 not-prose">
  <div class="flex gap-4 mb-4 items-center">
    <div class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-brand-red text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-lg">2</div>
    <h3 class="text-xl font-serif text-brand-dark">Identify opportunities</h3>
  </div>
  <p class="text-stone-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">After understanding AI's possibilities, look for where it can add value. Start with your pain points, not with the technology.</p>
  <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-4">
    <div class="p-4 bg-white border border-stone-200">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-brand-dark mb-2">Keep an AI logbook</h4>
      <p class="text-sm text-stone-600">For two weeks, note every time you think: "this takes too much time" or "this is boring work".</p>
    </div>
    <div class="p-4 bg-white border border-stone-200">
      <h4 class="font-bold text-brand-dark mb-2">Analyze processes</h4>
      <p class="text-sm text-stone-600">Step back and look at your workflow. Where are the bottlenecks? Where are resources being wasted?</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mb-12 not-prose">
  <div class="flex gap-4 mb-4 items-center">
    <div class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-brand-red text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-lg">3</div>
    <h3 class="text-xl font-serif text-brand-dark">Analyze opportunities</h3>
  </div>
  <p class="text-stone-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">When opportunities are clear, the next step is the business case. What does it cost? What does it deliver? Be honest with yourself.</p>
  <div class="bg-stone-50 p-6 border-l-4 border-brand-red">
    <ul class="space-y-2 text-stone-600 list-disc ml-5">
      <li><strong>Estimate costs:</strong> Licenses, implementation, training, maintenance</li>
      <li><strong>Calculate returns:</strong> Time savings, quality improvement, new capabilities</li>
      <li><strong>Create project plan:</strong> Timeline, capabilities, expectations</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mb-12 not-prose">
  <div class="flex gap-4 mb-4 items-center">
    <div class="flex-shrink-0 w-10 h-10 bg-brand-red text-white rounded-full flex items-center justify-center font-bold text-lg">4</div>
    <h3 class="text-xl font-serif text-brand-dark">Implement</h3>
  </div>
  <p class="text-stone-600 leading-relaxed mb-4">With a solid plan, implementation is the next step. You have two options: buy an existing solution or build something custom. In both cases, integration is the challenge.</p>
  <div class="dark-box-content bg-brand-dark p-8 border-l-4 border-brand-red">
    <h4 class="font-serif mb-3">It's about people</h4>
    <p class="leading-relaxed">Technical integration is one thing. But the real challenge is getting your team on board. People are naturally resistant to change. Involve them early, gather input, make sure they feel supported. This makes the difference between success and failure.</p>
  </div>
</div>
